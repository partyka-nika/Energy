About website

This site was created in order to highlight the situation with energy carriers at the moment. After all, every day we use one or another type of energy, that is, the planet's resources are used every day and, unfortunately, most of them are exhaustible.
To prevent a terrible catastrophe in the near future, humanity should replace exhaustible energy sources with inexhaustible ones, thereby causing much less damage to the environment.
On this site you will find examples of these alternative resources and concrete advice on what each of us can do right now for this important cause.
I hope you will find your time here not only useful but also interesting!
